# Tayyab Snacks — Vision & Mission

## Name Origin
**Tayyab** (طيب) means pure, wholesome, and good in Arabic. In Islamic dietary law, food must be both **Halal** (permissible) and **Tayyab** (pure, clean, healthy, ethical). Most snack brands only chase halal certification; Tayyab Snacks ensures both halal AND tayyab — clean ingredients, no harmful additives.

## Vision
To build the go-to community-funded platform for halal, additive-free snack and packaged food entrepreneurs — helping Muslim families trust what they feed their children, and helping small tayyab snack makers get the capital to grow.

## Mission
Tayyab Snacks is a contribution (crowdfunding) platform focused specifically on **packaged and snack food** campaigns — from home-based snack makers launching their first product line, to small halal confectionery and bakery brands scaling production — funded directly by the Muslim community.

## Tagline
**"Pure Snacks. Pure Intentions. Pure Community."**

---

## Core Values
1. **Tayyab Standard** — No artificial colours, no pork-derived gelatin, no harmful preservatives.
2. **Community Trust** — Every campaign is reviewed before going live; funds tracked transparently.
3. **Support the Small Maker** — Prioritise home-based and small-batch producers over big snack brands.
4. **Transparency** — Ingredients, sourcing, and fund usage are disclosed publicly.
5. **Barakah Economy** — Encouraging Muslims to invest in a clean halal snack supply chain.

---

## Target Audience
- **Contributors:** Muslim parents and consumers who want trustworthy snack options
- **Campaign Creators:** Home bakers, small snack brands, confectioners, dried-fruit/nut packers, halal beverage makers
- **Consumers:** Anyone seeking a directory of verified tayyab packaged snacks

---

## What Is a Campaign?
A campaign is a funding request from a snack/packaged-food maker:
- "Help us launch our halal kids' snack line" — first production run
- "Fund our halal certification for our bakery brand" — certification cost
- "Scale our dried fruit & nut packaging business" — equipment/packaging
- "Launch our tayyab juice and beverage brand" — bottling equipment

---

## Key Features (MVP)
- **Campaign Listings:** Browse active snack-campaigns with goal, raised amount, deadline
- **Contribution:** Contribute any amount to a campaign (recorded with donor info)
- **Campaign Submission:** Any registered user can submit a campaign for review
- **User Dashboard:** Track your contributions and your submitted campaigns
- **Snack Directory:** (Planned) List of verified tayyab snack brands by city
- **Progress Tracking:** Real-time progress bar showing funds raised vs. goal
- **Social Sharing:** Share campaigns to WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.
